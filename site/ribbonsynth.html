RibbonSynth

Use your mouse to play. Horizontal movement changes the pitch, and vertical movement changes the volume. Press 1-5 to change the waveform (p5.sound's implementation of pulse wave oscillators is buggy, so 5 may not work as expected). Hold O to go up an octave. Press Q to toggle pitch quantization. Press R to toggle recording.
